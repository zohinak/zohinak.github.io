British reign
on Kenya.

The British Empire established the East Africa Protectorate
in 1895, from 1920 known as the Kenya Colony.Between
1896 and 1901, some 32,000 indentured labourers were
recruited from India to construct the Uganda railway. Work
on the railways was brutally hard. By the time it had
reached its destination in Kisumu on the banks of Lake
Victoria, roughly 625 miles from Mombasa,around 2,500
men had died. That's four people for every mile.
Indian slaves were brought
to Africa to build a railroad
from Kenya to Uganda.

Indian influence
on Kenya.

Indians brought their favorite ingredients and spices to Kenya
when they were brought here by the British. Many of what we
know today as traditional kenyan Dishes, are actually made possible
because of Specific ingredients Indians used, and taught Kenyans
how to use. Some of these Ingredients have become Kenyan favorites! Learn which ingredients those are!
