What is "CHAKULA" about?

CHAKULA shows the fusion between Indian and Kenyan cuisine.
Many people who are familiar with both kenyan and indian
foods, do not know how they are interrelated! Learn more about the
similarities and differences between Kenyan and Indian food and culture!
Click on the countries to always go back to the Indian and Kenyan pages.

EVERY INDIAN
KITCHEN NEEDS:

  • Spices of course!
  • Pots for curries, and pans for deep frying.
  • Lots and lots of containers.
  • Vegetable oil, and ghee to cook with.

EVERY KENYAN
KITCHEN NEEDS:

  • Yeast and flour.
  • Lots of storage for legumes, and vegetables.
  • Vegetables, beans, and beef.
  • Stainless steel pots to cook over the fire.
